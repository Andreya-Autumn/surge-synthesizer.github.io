Effects

8 effect units arranged as 2 inserts per scene, 2 sends and 2 master effects.

24 effect algorithms:

3-band EQ, 11-band Graphic EQ, Exciter, Resonator, CHOW, Distortion, Neuron, Tape, Combulator, Frequency Shifter, Nimbus (a famous Eurorack live input granularizer), Ring Modulator, Treemonster (pitch-tracked ring modulator from Shortcircuit 2!), Vocoder, Chorus, Ensemble (BBD based or clean!), Flanger, Phaser, Rotary Speaker, Delay, Reverb 1, Reverb 2, Conditioner (low/high EQ, stereo image and limiter) and Airwindows (itself containing 53 wonderful effects of various kinds!)
